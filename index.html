Artist Statement

The silence between people — the presence that isn't fully felt. My work explores how we live beside one another, yet remain distant. In a world of constant connection, I'm drawn to the quiet gaps — between screens, between gestures, between rooms.

My paintings emerge from a still place, where emotions drift unspoken, and nearness doesn't always mean closeness. These are not answers, but emotional pauses — soft reflections on what it means to be here, and still feel alone.

Gallery
