Kajal Chaudhary

MSU · Final Year

“Exploring stories through colors, forms & spaces.”

Artist Statement

My work is a reflection of emotion, identity, and space. Each piece is an invitation into the inner worlds we often overlook. Through texture, form, and quiet symbolism, I aim to capture what often goes unspoken.

About Me

I’m an interdisciplinary artist studying at Srishti Manipal. I enjoy blending digital and traditional mediums to craft unique, thought-provoking visuals that explore memory, culture, and place. My creative practice is rooted in observation, curiosity, and storytelling.

Gallery

A curated collection of visuals that explore memory, identity, and space — fragments of stories told through form and color.
